About the platform

City Finder is a cutting-edge platform that helps users discover and explore cities across the globe. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use, comprehensive tool that allows users to access information on various cities, including local attractions, public transportation, and nearby amenities.

By leveraging advanced mapping technology and up-to-date data, City Finder aims to enhance users' travel experiences and assist in making informed decisions when planning their next trip.

Meet The Founder

Noel Oduory

A seasoned traveler, software engineer and investment analyst, founded City Finder after recognizing the need for a single, unified platform to explore new cities. Combining his passion for travel and expertise in web development, Noel created City Finder to bridge the gap between wanderlust and technology. His vision is to continuously improve the platform, providing users with the most accurate and relevant information to enrich their travel experiences.
